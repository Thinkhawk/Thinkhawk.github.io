Relief Work

After a survey conducted it was found that many weren't aware of the education loans and various scholarships that they were eligible for which could make their lives much more easier.

UDHAVU ensures that they are well acknowledged about the education loans that they could take for the further studies of their wards and also we act as a bridge between them and the bank in order to help them with all the formalities. We also help those eligible for scholarships apply for them, and ensure that they get the said amount in the said time.
