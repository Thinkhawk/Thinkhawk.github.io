Relief Work

UDHAVU intends to make education accessible to all people irrespective of their background. In the present scenario, with the growing competition it is extremely hard to get a stand unless you are educated. A number of volunteers from various places join hands with us in order to help us succeed in our objectives.

Volunteers are sent to these rural areas weekly twice to provide the kids with the basic education that they require. Looking into the keen interest of a adults of a few rural areas in getting the minimum education we also started giving them classes and helping them make their lives a little more easier.
