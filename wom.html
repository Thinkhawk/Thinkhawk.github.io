INTERNATIONAL WOMENS DAY

UDHAVU marks International Women’s Day every year to celebrate the cause of women empowerment, especially women and girls from marginalised communities. This year, in line with United Nation’s theme for International Women’s Day (IWD) on March 8th – #BeBoldForChange, UDHAVU joins the movement to accelerate gender parity across the world, by sharing bold actions that have been have undertaken so far, or that need to be taken in the future both at an individual and organizational level to progress the gender agenda. UDHAVU is organizing an event – “International Women’s Day #BeBoldForChange : Reducing Gender Based Violence for Empowerment of Women” on March 7, 2020 India Habitat Centre to create a forum for discussion around Gender Based Violence (GBV) in India. Several peer organizations will participate in the event and share their experiences/perspectives on working towards eliminating GBV against women. The event will host an exclusive session on various experiences/expertise around work on Domestic Violence and status of the Protection for Women against Domestic Violence (PWDV) Act implementation across different parts of the country.
