Blood bank is the one place that comes to our mind only when there is an urgent requirement of blood (mostly at the time of surgeries and accidents). Even though there are people who are eligible to donate blood, going to blood banks for the sake of donation alone might seem not-so-feasible to the rural public considering the distance to be traveled and the expenses that come along.

UDHAVU,considering this made sure that the blood bank reaches them and gives them an opportunity help those in need. We also make sure that they aren't abandoned when in need by registering their names along with their blood groups so that they contact us for any blood requirements and we provide them with all the help that they need.
