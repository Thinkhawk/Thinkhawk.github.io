Education

Relief Work


Looking into the various surveys conducted in the rural localities it was found that in most cases of death due to any disease the patient either failed to identify the symptoms and what the disease was or couldnt get the required treatment at the right time due to the lack of facilities and the lack of knowledge about the treatments.

A basic general check-up is conducted for all once in 3 months after which looking into the results are refered to hospitals if any kind of treatment is required. As part of the medical camps, an awareness session is conducted which would cover a lot of diseases,their symptoms and the treatments available. We also make sure that they are well aware about medical insurances.
