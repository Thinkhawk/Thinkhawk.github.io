Relief Work


This global pandemic that has affected almost all countries in various ways largely affected daily wage workers who could no longer find a job. UDHAVU in collaboration with "For Humanity" distributed ration and food packets to them and other sections of the society who found it difficult to get 3 square meal a day. We also made sure that stray animals aren't left to starve.
